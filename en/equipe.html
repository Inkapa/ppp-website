Our Team

Liam CORNU

Team leader, web developer, editor and translator

Hi, my name is Liam, as the team leader I oversaw the group's management, the overall progress of the project and the organisation of our interview and website. I am responsible for the complete development of this website, its translation as well as the the direction and editing of the video «Je Filme un Métier qui Me Plait» and the translation of its subtitles.

Lucas LELEU

Writer and proofreader

Hello, y name is Lucas, and during the elaboration of this project I was in charge of the documentary research for the different parts of this website, in particular for Home page with its quick presentation of the job we worked on. I was therefore in charge of researching information on our job as well as proofreading and correcting the textual elements (in French) developed by Liam and Sébastien.

Jules GUYARD

Graphics controller

My name is Jules, and my role during the progress of this project was to control the visual aspect of our site. I evaluated and gave criticism on the visual choices and ideas of each group member. Enzo CARDOSO

Organisation secretary

Hi, I’m Enzo, and I was in charge of taking notes during our meetings. I also had to make up a back-schedule and a to-do list. Sébastien NICOLLE
